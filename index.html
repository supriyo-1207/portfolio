About Me

Hi there!

My name is Supriyo Maity, and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Applications (MCA) at the Calcutta Institute of Technology (CIT) in West Bengal. I previously completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA) from Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, West Bengal (formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology). I am deeply passionate about web development, cloud computing, and generative AI.

I specialize in developing modern, responsive web applications with a strong emphasis on user experience and performance. Through my work with OpenAI’s API and LangChain, I have built advanced natural language processing systems and explored various large language models (LLMs), gaining a deeper understanding of their diverse applications. Currently, I am expanding my expertise into cloud computing, particularly with AWS, to create scalable, secure solutions that address the evolving needs of the tech landscape.

I am committed to continuous learning, staying updated with the latest trends and best practices in web development and cloud computing. I enjoy tackling new challenges and collaborating with others to create innovative, impactful solutions. My goal is to build applications that not only solve real-world problems but also have a meaningful impact.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading about emerging technologies, experimenting with new programming languages, and contributing to open-source projects. I’m enthusiastic about the future of technology and excited by the endless opportunities it offers to solve complex problems and deliver exceptional user experiences.
